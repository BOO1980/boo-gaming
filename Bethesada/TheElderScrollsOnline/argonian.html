Argonians

Overview

  • The Argonians are possessed of a cool intellect.
  • Well-versed in the magical arts, stealth and the use of blades
  • They are guerilla warfare experts
  • They are long accustomed to defending their borders from invaders
  • They often serve as the scouts and skirmishers for the forces of the Pact
  • Nagas are a "subspecies" of Argonians found further south in places like Murkmire
  • The racial skills of the Argonians reflect their people's perilous swamp environment; giving them bonuses to healing done, restoration staves, disease resistance, resources gained from consuming potions, swimming speed, as well as base health and magicka
